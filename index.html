01 // ABOUT

Who I Am

I am an Electrical & Electronic Engineer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh with a B.Sc. from Independent University, Bangladesh and extensive hands-on experience in industrial control systems. In mid-2026 I completed a rigorous 272-hour, competency-based PLC and automation programme at SIMT under the ADB-funded SICIP initiative — a course structured around live equipment, not simulations.

My practical work spans PLC programming on both Wecon LX3V and Siemens S7-1200 platforms, analog I/O signal conditioning, PID loop commissioning, VFD configuration for motor speed control, and HMI development in PiStudio. I have also designed and evaluated solar PV systems and built Power BI dashboards for operational reporting.

In July 2026 I joined Sea-Lion Maritime Technology, Chittagong as Assistant Engineer (Electrical). I am interested in roles where electrical systems have real consequences — marine, industrial, or energy — and where precision and reliability are non-negotiable.
